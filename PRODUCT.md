# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Affluent travelers considering private, high-touch Rajasthan journeys. They arrive with taste, money, and limited patience for generic travel packaging. They want confidence that the trip will feel personal, discreet, atmospheric, and expertly paced.

## Product Purpose

TIV Rajasthan presents a quieter luxury travel service for Rajasthan: palace access, desert light, cultural encounters, and private itineraries composed around the traveler's pace. Success means a visitor feels trust, desire, and specificity quickly enough to start a conversation with a travel designer.

## Brand Personality

Private, cinematic, precise. Quiet luxury leads, with controlled moments of royal scale and visual drama. The brand should feel edited, sensory, and deliberate rather than loud or ornamental.

## Anti-references

- Package-tour clutter: discount banners, overloaded itinerary grids, generic travel agency conversion patterns.
- Hotel beige blandness: safe luxury neutrals without Rajasthan atmosphere, memory, or point of view.
- Maharaja cliché: costume-palace tropes, fake heritage signals, loud gold-and-maroon excess.
- Tech startup polish: SaaS gradients, icon-card sections, dashboard-like conversion mechanics.

## Design Principles

- Make privacy visible through restraint: show access and care without shouting exclusivity.
- Let Rajasthan carry the drama: use place, light, texture, and image rhythm before decorative ornament.
- Edit every section like an itinerary: each fold should have one clear mood and one reason to continue.
- Keep luxury tactile and specific: rooms, routes, meals, timing, and atmosphere beat abstract promises.
- Convert by conversation, not pressure: calls to action should feel like an invitation from a trusted planner.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA. Maintain strong contrast over imagery, keyboard-accessible navigation and calls to action, descriptive alt text, and readable line lengths. Respect reduced-motion preferences, especially for parallax, scroll reveals, and repeated motion.
