# PRODUCT.md - TIV Rajasthan

## Users
- **Primary:** Foreign luxury travelers seeking highly personalized, exclusive experiences in Rajasthan.
- **Secondary:** Domestic ultra-luxury guests who value attention to detail and premium hospitality.

## Brand
- **Name:** TIV Rajasthan
- **Identity:** A premier private tour operator that doesn't just "sell packages" but curates life-changing experiences.
- **Values:** Trust, Exclusivity, Curiosity, Comfort, Premium Hospitality.

## Tone & Voice
- **Premium & Immersive:** The language should be evocative, focusing on the *essence* and *feeling* of a destination rather than just its history.
- **Minimal & Intentional:** No bulky text, no clutter. Every word earns its place.
- **Professional & Serious:** Building credibility through high-end presentation and clear storytelling.

## Strategic Principles
- **Experience-First:** We sell memories and emotions, not itineraries and hotel rooms.
- **"Anti Gravity" Aesthetic:** Smooth, weightless transitions and animations that feel sophisticated and effortless.
- **Confusion-Free Navigation:** Organized, well-defined sections that keep the visitor engaged without overwhelm.

## Anti-References
- Bulky descriptions and unnecessary messaging.
- Spammy or "cheap" travel agency elements (e.g., flashy "Book Now" buttons, cluttered grids).
- Generic SaaS-style metrics or flat, uninspired layouts.
